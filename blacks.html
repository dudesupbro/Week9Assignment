The Midway Chair

Showdown is home to some of the best black diamond skiing in Montana.
We may not have the steepest, nor the longest blacks, but our ridge is quite fun for even the most advanced.
The kicker: our best black diamonds are immediately serviced by a mid-way chairlift.
This means that you can lap some fun, challenging black diamonds with a 5 minute chairlift ride.

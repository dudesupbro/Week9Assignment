About Showdown

Showdown is the oldest ski hill in Montana.
In fact, it is one of the oldest in the United States.
What began as Kings Hill Ski Area in 1936, has grown to one of the most family-friendly ski hills in Montana.
With consistent snow and challenging black diamonds serviced by a mid-way chair, Showdown has begun to attract skiers from Bozeman due to crowding at Bridger Bowl and Big Sky.
